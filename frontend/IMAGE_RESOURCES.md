```jsx

<img src={heroImage} alt="Volunteers" />
```

## Tips

1. **Optimize images**: Add `?w=1200&q=80` to control width and quality
2. **Responsive**: Use different sizes for mobile/desktop
3. **Lazy loading**: Add `loading="lazy"` to img tags
4. **Alt text**: Always include descriptive alt text for accessibility
5. **Cache**: Consider downloading and serving from your own CDN for better performance
